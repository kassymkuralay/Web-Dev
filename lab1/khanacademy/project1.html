China

China is a vast country with a long history and rich cultural traditions. It is known for its ancient cities, modern skylines, and diverse landscapes ranging from mountains to rivers.
Visitors can experience unique cuisine, historic landmarks, and everyday life that blends tradition with modern development.

Things to see

  • The Great Wall – a historic structure stretching across northern China
  • The Forbidden City – an imperial palace complex in Beijing
  • The Terracotta Army – ancient clay soldiers located in Xi’an
  • The Bund– a famous waterfront area in Shanghai
  • Zhangjiajie National Forest Park
  • The Yellow Mountains (Huangshan)

Places to go

Visitors can explore historic cities, visit ancient temples and palaces, and enjoy modern urban life in large cities. Nature lovers can hike in mountain areas, take river cruises, and admire scenic landscapes found across different regions of China
