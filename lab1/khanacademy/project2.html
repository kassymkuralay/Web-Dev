Recipe #1: Classic Pancakes

  • Flour — 1 cup
  • Milk — 1 cup
  • Egg — 1
  • Sugar — 1 tbsp
  • Baking powder — 1 tsp
  • Salt — a pinch
Details:
Time: 15–20 minutes
Servings: 2–3
Difficulty: Easy
  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add milk and egg, then mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan and lightly oil it.
  4. Pour small circles of batter and cook until bubbles appear.
  5. Flip and cook the other side for 1–2 minutes.

Recipe #3: Fruit Yogurt Bowl

  • Yogurt
  • Banana
  • Berries
  • Honey
  • Granola
Details:
Time: 5 minutes
Servings: 1
Difficulty: Very easy
  1. Put yogurt in a bowl.
  2. Slice banana and add fruit on top.
  3. Add honey if you want it sweeter.
  4. Sprinkle granola or nuts
  5. Serve immediately.
